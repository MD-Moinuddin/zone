WHAT WE DO

For Tier 0-1 productions, we offer Color Correction services, ensuring that your footage reaches its full potential and enhances the visual storytelling of your narratives. Our team is dedicated to transforming your raw footage into extraordinary experiences, elevating the overall quality of your projects.

Whether you need assistance with post-production tasks or are looking for a reliable team to shoot special events, ZONE PRODUCTIONS has the skills and capabilities to meet your needs. Our goal is to deliver exceptional results that exceed your expectations, making your projects stand out in the competitive world of Film and TV production.

TRAILERS

BEAUTIFUL, ORGANIZED CUTS.

ZONE PRODUCTIONS creates exciting Trailers for Feature Films, Documentaries, Commercials and TV Dramas. Our team was created to produce quality production pieces to aid our clients with marketable material to promote and sell their concepts, TV shows and films.
Character, tone, setting, genre, and originality are all required for a good trailer. Our goal is to make viewers want to watch the rest of the movie. You will receive high art technical editing with beautiful pacing and emotional depth.

COLOR CORRECTION / GRADING

COLOR MOOD.

Our team of colorists bring decades of experience and technical know how to get the perfect look for your footage. We provide effective strategies and an efficient workflow tailored to your production and color management needs, whether it be for film or television.
On set color correction allows quick color adjustments to be made to the image by the colorist allowing them and others to see the image in a way much closer to how it will actually look on the screen of your choice.
